MoChat is a motion-centric multimodal large language model for multi-turn motion comprehension, spatio-temporal grounding, and natural-language description.

Key publication details:

* First published online: November 10, 2025
* Journal issue: IEEE JBHI, vol. 30, no. 3, pp. 1972-1985 (March 2026)
* DOI: [10.1109/JBHI.2025.3631045](https://doi.org/10.1109/JBHI.2025.3631045)
* Links: [Publisher Page](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11237042) | [arXiv preprint](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.11404) | [Code](https://github.com/CSUBioGroup/MoChat)

In this work, We contributed to skeleton format selection, encoder design, dataset preparation for NTU RGB+D, annotation generation with CogVLM, and the temporal regression module used for spatio-temporal localization and description.
